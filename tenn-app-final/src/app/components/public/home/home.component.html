TEEN PROBLEMS
It’s not easy being a teenager in today’s world. From depression to bullying and cutting, teens grapple with a wide range of issues as they grow and develop. But here’s the good news: you’re not alone and you can feel better.
Learn more about the signs and symptoms of some common teen challenges, what you can do to help yourself, and how to reach out and talk about tough subjects with your friends and family members.

PARENT HELPER
Parenting has often been described as a full-time job. Moms and dads have to organize children’s schedules, find kid-friendly places to eat, and remember all the things that their family needs from the grocery store this week. Fortunately, there is TEEN@home app that can help busy parents to keep track of everything.
We are here to help parents with all parenting challenges from those everyday situations to the most serious issue of child abuse.
